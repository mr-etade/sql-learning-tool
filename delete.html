DELETE Command

Remove records from a database table

Basic DELETE

Remove a specific student:

DELETE FROM Students WHERE StudentID = 108;

Conditional DELETE

Delete students with grade F:

DELETE FROM Students WHERE Grade = 'F';

Delete with NULL Condition

Delete students with no grade:

DELETE FROM Students WHERE Grade IS NULL;
